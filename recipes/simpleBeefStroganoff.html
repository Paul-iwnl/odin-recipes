Simple Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients :

  1. 1 pound beef sirloin or tenderloin, thinly sliced
  2. 1 small onion, finely chopped
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  5. 2 tablespoons butter
  6. 1 cup beef broth
  7. 1 cup sour cream
  8. 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  9. 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  10. Salt and pepper to taste
  11. 8 ounces egg noodles or your choice of pasta

Instructions :

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced beef and cook until it's no longer pink. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the butter, chopped onion, garlic, and mushrooms. Cook until they are tender.
  4. Sprinkle the flour over the mushroom mixture and stir to combine.
  5. Gradually stir in the beef broth and cook until the sauce thickens.
  6. Return the cooked beef to the skillet, and simmer for a few minutes.
  7. Stir in the sour cream and heat through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the beef stroganoff over cooked noodles.
