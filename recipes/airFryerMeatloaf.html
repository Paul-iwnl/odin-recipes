Air Fryer Chicken:

Ingredients :

  1. 1 pound ground beef
  2. 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  3. 1/4 cup milk
  4. 1/4 cup ketchup
  5. 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  6. 1/4 cup finely chopped bell pepper
  7. 1 egg
  8. 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  9. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  10. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  11. Cooking spray

Instructions :

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, ketchup, chopped onion, chopped bell pepper, egg, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and black pepper. Mix until well combined.
